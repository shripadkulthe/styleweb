A charming ocean front resort with 38 hotel rooms, studios and suites, most with balcony and jacuzzi, located directly on the Turquoise water of the Atlantic Ocean. Sample Resort offers you the opportunity to relax and enjoy a wonderful beachside Caribbean vacation in beautiful ABC City

Start the day relaxing by the ocean under our palm trees, as you enjoy our complimentary newspaper. Also be sure to enjoy a nice swim in our heated 50,000 gallon fresh water pool or walk on our expansive beach. Want to enjoy something unique? Join the snorklers and divers exploring our most revered treasure, the three natural living coral reefs that are just 100 yards off-shore. The Sample Resort is the premier hotel for this rare and beautiful natural wonder
